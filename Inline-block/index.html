Barcelona beat Atletico, but fall short of Copa del Rey final

Atlético Madrid lost 3–0 to FC Barcelona on Tuesday in the second leg of their Copa del Rey semi-final. However, because Atlético won the first game 4–0, they advanced to the final 4–3 on aggregate. Barcelona tried hard to complete an incredible comeback at Camp Nou. Young player Marc Bernal scored twice, and Raphinha added a penalty. Despite the win on the night, it was not enough to overturn the heavy first-leg defeat.
